The Kensington Group is an established meeting and event services company specializing in all areas of meeting and event production.

Founded on critical, contemporary measures of performance, cost, quality and service, we are an independent company, headquartered in Raleigh, NC.

The Kensington Group takes great pride in understanding the dynamic business issues of our customers, specifically their goals and objectives and the message they want to convey to their audience.

As a strategic partner with our clients, it is our commitment to share in their success by executing our responsibilities in such a way that nothing interferes with the delivery and receptivity to this message.

We strive to provide leadership, maintain strong industry relationships, and reach 100% customer satisfaction.

Our ideal is to manage all program logistics with flawless attention to detail, meet all timelines, supply accurate reports, and implement "best practices" and service standards.

The Kensington Group’s extensive experience with meetings and events helps to strengthen our knowledge and understanding of your goals and objectives.

This experience allows us to collaborate with our clients and strategically implement a quality and prestigious meeting.

Nearly 25 years of meeting experience allows us to design and plan the perfect event for you.
